# Long-Term Vision: 2030-2035

## Executive Summary

Savitri Network's long-term vision extends beyond being a blockchain platform to becoming the foundational infrastructure for a truly decentralized digital society. By 2035, we envision a world where Savitri Network powers billions of daily interactions across finance, governance, identity, and social coordination.

## Strategic Objectives

### 2030: Foundation Phase
- **1M Daily Active Users**: Establish critical mass for network effects
- **10,000 Active Validators**: Achieve global decentralization
- **1000+ Applications**: Build diverse ecosystem of services
- **Cross-Chain Integration**: Connect with major blockchain networks

### 2032: Expansion Phase
- **10M Daily Active Users**: Reach mainstream adoption
- **Enterprise Integration**: Major corporations using Savitri for critical operations
- **DeFi Leadership**: Become primary platform for decentralized finance
- **Identity Standard**: Establish Savitri ID as global digital identity solution

### 2035: Maturity Phase
- **100M Daily Active Users**: Achieve mass-market adoption
- **Government Adoption**: Multiple governments using Savitri for public services
- **Infrastructure Layer**: Become utility-grade infrastructure like internet protocols
- **Complete Decentralization**: No single entity controls more than 1% of network power
